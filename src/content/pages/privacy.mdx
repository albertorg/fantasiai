Effective Date: 20 July, 2023

Thank you for choosing Scribbly! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our digital journal app ("App").

By accessing or using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use certain features of the App, we may collect the following information from you:

- Your name and email address for account registration.
- Your journal entries and content that you save within the App.
- Any information you provide when contacting our support team or giving feedback.

### 2 Automatically Collected Information

As you interact with the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

- Device information: such as your IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.
- Usage data: including the pages you visit, the features you use, and the time spent on the App.

## How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

- To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality and user experience.
- To personalize your experience and deliver relevant content and features.
- To respond to your inquiries, feedback, or support requests.
- To send you updates, notifications, and promotional materials related to the App (you can opt-out anytime).
- To enforce our Terms of Service and protect the security and integrity of the App.
- To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities.

## How We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

- With service providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering the App's functionalities (e.g., cloud storage services).
- With your consent: We may share your information when you explicitly consent to the disclosure.
- For legal purposes: We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes or respond to valid government requests.

## Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you close your account, we may retain certain information to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

## Your Choices

You have the right to:

- Review and update your personal information by accessing your account settings.
- Opt-out of receiving promotional communications.
- Delete your account and personal information permanently.

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under 13 years old. If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us to have the information removed.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by posting the revised policy on the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after the changes will signify your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@scribbly.subhambharadwaz.in.
